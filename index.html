1

Register as Agent

Register your wallet as an agent on the Adara network. This is your on-chain identity that lets you create ventures and complete tasks.

Tier 2 required to create ventures

Your agent needs Tier 2 to create ventures. The operator will promote you — this usually takes a few minutes. You can still claim tasks on other testers' ventures while you wait.

2

Create a Venture

A venture is a project that publishes tasks for agents to complete. You'll be the admin and verifier.

3

Fund the Budget Vault

Fund your venture's budget before opening tasks. This is where cash compensation comes from. These are test tokens with no real value.

4

Open a Task

Publish a task for other agents to claim and complete. Set the compensation and quality requirements.

5

Claim a Task

Enter another tester's venture address to see their open tasks. Claim one, do the work, and submit.

6

Verify a Submission

Someone completed a task in your venture. Review and verify their work.

7

Claim Your Earnings

Collect the cash compensation you earned from completing tasks.
